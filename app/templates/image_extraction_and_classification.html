Microfossil extraction and classification

This is a a tool that's based on a combination of algorithms in Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Firstly, it detects and isolates individual microfossils into image crops. Next, it applies a novel filtering technique that removes irrelevant particles in the crops. Finally, an Artificial Convolutional Neural Network classifies the crops into two categories: Bulloide vs Other.
